ABOUT ME

My name is Daniel Grant, I am a professional Wakeboarder and Wakeskater living in Bangkok, Thailand. I have been riding since the age of 8 and turned professional at the age of 13. My website is to give information what I am up to, as well as some highlights from my career so far. in both 2015 and 2016 I have ran my own video projects (2015 - TAO + 2016 - Eclipse), both videos and background behind them can be found within the website. This as well as other video projects I have been part of. Many are freeview and can be found on youtube or vimeo, a number you can purchse as a blueray DVD or via Itunes. After 2016 and my ECLIPSE edit, I have two ideas for this year and I will work closely with Rip Curl and SPY, as I did with Eclipse. The first concept is a locaiton search. The first two countries will be New Zealand and Ireland. Probably the two most beautiful countires in the world that I have never visited in the past. They are also unique as they are “new” wake markets, especially for riding cable. We will hit spots mostly on a winch. Another concept as a GROM SEARCH. I will be working together with my sponsors, and wanted to pass through a numebr of cable parks and ride together with some of the upcomig Groms. The level of riding right now with kids is insane, and I want to spend time with the upcoming riders and give them a chance to show what they have got. The GROM Search will start in March, with the first stop being Bali - Indonesia.
